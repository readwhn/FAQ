Frequently Asked Questions(FAQ)

How long do chickens live?

When shielded from predators they tend to live 5-10years depending on their breeds

What do i need for my new chicks?

Your new chicks must stay warm and cared for until after first weeks. A brooder with a single infared lamp is the best and most economical choice.

Are chicken noisy?

When you have roosters, you have noise and roosters not only crow in the morning, but all day long . Hens, on the other hand are quieter

What is the best set up for day old chicks?

Set up the brooder (Brooder is the first home of new chicks) Be sure it is comfortable, warm and daft-free with at least 3 to 4 square feet per chick.

Do day old chicks need sunlight?

Baby chicks needs 24 hours of light for the first 48 to 72 hours of life to ensure that they findfood and water.
